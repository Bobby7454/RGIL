About the College

A Tradition of
Legal Scholarship

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Law stands as one of Andhra Pradesh's most respected institutions for legal education. Situated at 4-15, Ramanayyapeta, Kakinada, the college is affiliated to Adikavi Nannaya University and recognised by the Bar Council of India — the apex regulatory body for legal education in India.

Our institution offers two BCI-approved programmes — the 3-Year LL.B and the integrated 5-Year BA LL.B — designed to cultivate not just legal knowledge, but also analytical thinking, ethical judgment, and a deep sense of public service.

With a faculty comprising experienced legal scholars and practising advocates, a well-stocked legal library, a state-of-the-art moot court hall, and an active legal aid programme, RGIL provides students with everything they need to thrive as tomorrow's legal professionals.

Our Story

A Journey of Purpose

What began as a vision in Kakinada has grown into a journey of resilience and transformation—shaping futures, empowering minds, and building a legacy in legal education across Coastal Andhra Pradesh.

The Beginning

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Law was founded with a simple yet powerful belief—that every aspiring student deserves access to quality legal education. In its early days, the path was challenging, but with determination and a clear purpose, the foundation for a lasting institution was laid.

Est.
BCI

A Defining Milestone

Recognition from the Bar Council of India marked a turning point in our journey. It was more than an approval—it was a testament to our commitment to quality, credibility, and the highest standards in legal education.

Strengthening Foundations

With affiliation to Adikavi Nannaya University, the institution gained academic strength and structure. This step opened new doors for students, ensuring recognised degrees and a solid platform to build successful legal careers.

AkNU
LLB

Expanding Opportunities

Understanding the diverse aspirations of students, RGIL introduced both the 5-Year B.A. LL.B. integrated programme for fresh graduates of Class XII and the 3-Year LL.B. programme for those seeking to enter the legal profession after completing their degree. This expansion opened multiple pathways into the world of law.

Learning Beyond Classrooms

At RGIL, education extends beyond textbooks. With a fully equipped moot court hall and an active Legal Aid Cell, students engage with real-world legal challenges—building confidence, skill, and a deep sense of responsibility towards society.

Now

Continuing the Journey

Today, Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Law stands as a symbol of growth, perseverance, and possibility. With dedicated faculty, accomplished alumni, and ambitious students, the institution continues to move forward—shaping not just legal professionals, but responsible leaders of tomorrow.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Grounded in a deep belief in justice, equality, and the rule of law — our vision and mission guide every decision we make as an institution.

Our Vision

What we strive to be

To be a centre of excellence in legal education that produces competent, ethical, and socially responsible legal professionals who uphold the rule of law and serve the cause of justice in India and beyond.

We aspire to nurture legal thinkers who are as comfortable in a courtroom as they are in community service — advocates for both law and humanity.

  • Premier institution of legal education in coastal Andhra Pradesh
  • Nationally recognised for academic rigour and professional output
  • A model for ethical legal practice and community service
  • Driving legal literacy and access to justice for all

Our Mission

What we commit to do

To uphold the rule of law by educating future legal professionals with a strong foundation in justice, ethics, and public service — empowering them to transform society through informed and compassionate leadership.

We are committed to providing an environment where academic excellence, practical skills, and moral integrity flourish together.

  • Deliver BCI-compliant, rigorous legal education across all programmes
  • Develop practical courtroom and advocacy skills through moot courts
  • Foster community engagement through legal aid and NSS activities
  • Maintain a faculty of experienced scholars and legal practitioners
  • Provide internship and placement support for every graduate
Leadership

Chairman's Message

Chairman

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Law

Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh

"The study of law is not merely the study of statutes — it is the study of human society, its aspirations for justice, and its mechanisms for resolving conflict with reason and fairness."

It is with immense pride and deep sense of responsibility that I welcome you to the Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Law. Since our inception, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to making legal education meaningful, accessible, and transformative for the students of this region.

Legal education in India is at a pivotal juncture. Courts across the country face enormous backlogs; access to legal aid remains limited for many citizens; and new challenges — from technology law to environmental justice — demand a new generation of lawyers who are not only learned in doctrine, but also agile, empathetic, and purpose-driven.

At RGIL, we have deliberately built an environment where students don't simply learn the law — they learn to think, argue, and act like true advocates of justice. Our experienced faculty, our moot court facility, our legal aid camps, and our partnerships with courts and institutions are all oriented toward one goal: sending out graduates who will make a genuine difference.

To our students — past, present, and future — I say this: the law is a noble calling. It demands your intellect, your integrity, and your courage. We are here to prepare you for all three. I invite you to make the most of every opportunity this institution offers, and to carry forward the values we stand for.

Chairman, RGIL
